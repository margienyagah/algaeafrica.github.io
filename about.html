About the Algae Africa Network

Building Africa's leading network for algae research, innovation, education and sustainable development.

Our Story

The Algae Africa Network (AAN) was established to unite researchers, students, entrepreneurs, industry leaders, policymakers and investors working to advance algae science and biotechnology across Africa.

Although algae offer enormous opportunities for food security, climate resilience, aquaculture, renewable energy, biotechnology, green industries and the blue economy, collaboration across Africa has remained limited.

AAN was founded to bridge this gap by creating a continental platform that promotes collaboration, education, innovation and partnerships, enabling algae-based solutions to contribute to sustainable development throughout Africa.
