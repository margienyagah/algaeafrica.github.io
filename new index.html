About Algae Africa Network

The Algae Africa Network (AAN) is a pan-African platform bringing together researchers, entrepreneurs, educators, industry leaders, policymakers, investors, and development partners to unlock the potential of algae for sustainable development.

Our mission is to accelerate collaboration, knowledge exchange, innovation, and commercialization of algae-based solutions that contribute to food security, climate resilience, environmental restoration, renewable bioeconomies, and inclusive economic growth across Africa.

Why Algae Matters for Africa

Food Security

Algae provides sustainable food, feed, fertilizers and nutritional solutions for Africa's growing population.

Climate Solutions

Supporting carbon capture, wastewater treatment, ecosystem restoration and climate adaptation.

Blue Bioeconomy

Driving innovation in biotechnology, pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, aquaculture and renewable industries.
