Mapping Africa's Algae Innovation Landscape

The Algae Africa Network has begun developing Africa's Algae Innovation Landscape 2026, the network's first flagship continental report. The publication brings together evidence from scientific research, universities, companies, government initiatives, and development programmes to provide the first comprehensive overview of Africa's algae ecosystem. The report will identify emerging research strengths, innovation hubs, commercial activities, investment opportunities, and the challenges shaping algae development across the continent. It aims to become a practical resource for researchers, entrepreneurs, policymakers, investors, and institutions working to build Africa's blue bioeconomy. Coming Soon
