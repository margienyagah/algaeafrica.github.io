About Us

Algae Africa Network (AAN) is a collaborative platform uniting researchers, entrepreneurs, policymakers, and investors to accelerate algae development across the continent.

Together, we are building Africa’s algae movement — from food security to climate resilience, from biorefineries to green industries.

Our Work / Focus Areas

  • 🌱 Sustainable Food & Agriculture – advancing algae-based proteins, feeds, and fertilizers to strengthen food security.
  • 🌍 Climate & Environment – leveraging algae for carbon capture, wastewater treatment, and ecosystem restoration.
  • 💡 Innovation & Entrepreneurship – supporting African innovators and startups developing algae-based products.
  • 🎓 Research & Education – creating spaces for knowledge exchange, training, and partnerships across Africa and beyond.
