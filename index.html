About AAN

The Algae Africa Network (AAN) is Africa's continental platform connecting researchers, educators, entrepreneurs, policymakers, investors and institutions working to advance algae science, innovation and the blue bioeconomy.

Through collaboration, education and partnerships, AAN is building Africa's algae innovation ecosystem for sustainable development.

Why Algae Matters in Africa

Food Security

Supporting sustainable food, feed and nutritional solutions.

Climate Solutions

Carbon capture, ecosystem restoration, wastewater treatment and climate resilience.
